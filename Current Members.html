The main role of the president and vice presidents of the RGU Gaming Society is to ensure the happieness and wellbeing of all those within the society. They have made the commitment to listen to any queries or concerns and ensure they do all they can to resolve any problems. As well as this they are also responsible for organising and running society events although it is possible for other members of the society to get involved and help out. If,as a member of the society you have any ideas for potential events which could be run or simply just want to offer your help, all you need to do is get in touch with one of the presidnets, either in person at an event or via their email which is listed by their personal profile.
